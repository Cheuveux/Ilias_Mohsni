I am Ilias, a 23 half french half Tunisian artist from Paris. I am currently a community manager and a videographer. I studied graphic design for 3 years, and I am passionate into documentary films, design and art in general !

I started documenting my family city in Tunisia the year after the diploma, and created my firt amateur documentary film : When a time goes, an other comes (‎يمشي زمان و يجي زمان in arabic) that you can find in the documentary film section of this website !Now, I would like to keep doing films about new things, new places, new persons, so I am open to all documentary projects !

During this same year, I started my job as a community manager for a Japanese antic seller named Koji. I edit videos, take pictures of the products he sells, developed the online selling, and manage the Instagram account.

Also, I am a member of an artist collective named arc+ in which we organize events about art, sell clothes, and just try to connect artists and people together. We created the group and the brand for the first time in Japan, where we ran a concept shop in Tokyo for a month, with a part for our clothing brand, and an other for an art gallery.
